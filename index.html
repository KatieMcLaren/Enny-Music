The Artist

Artist Enny is an up and coming rapper who is quickly climbing the charts with hits like "Peng Black Girls". Enitan Adepitan, who goes by the performance name, Enny gained her professional start by uploading EPs to Youtube. She quickly gained a following and is collaborating with well-known artists such as Jorja Smith.

With an increase in her success, her singles have garnered an increase in downloads across platforms such as Spotify. Enny currently has two albums, Under Twenty Five and We Go Again. Both of which have gained millions of plays on Spotify.
